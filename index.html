I completed my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering under supervision of professor Pan Hui and professor Tristan Bruad. My research covers Computational Politics, Human-Computer Interaction, and users' and community behaviors in relation to offline event.

If you are interested in these areas and want to collaborate with me, please feel free to send me an email.
